Flat Design

Flat design is not just some fly-by-night trend.


Why create an app with flat design?

Despite its sudden popularity, flat design is not just some fly-by-night trend. It’s a substantial approach to Web design that’s rooted in practicality, and necessity. The balance between aesthetics and usability reflected in flat design demonstrates that the principles behind the philosophy have true staying power.

Flat design is not an all-or-nothing practice. Its individual components can be used as well on their own as they can part of the greater system. Therefore, the flat design approach was best suited for the iDo app beacuse of the limited amount of space that would be utilized, as well as the ease of usability the design would lend.

The principles of flat design, as well as the 10 principles of Dieter Rams, have deeply impacted my ideology and can be seen in my graphic design, website design and front-end development work.
